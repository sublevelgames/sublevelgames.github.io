Hexholm is a fast, turn-based hex strategy game. Start from a single province and carve out a kingdom — recruit troops, claim the map hex by hex, and crush rival AI factions before they overrun you. Every battle is a tight tactical puzzle: manage your gold, position your units, and time your pushes to expand without overextending.

Take on 100 hand-picked campaign stages, fight for the top of the global leaderboard in the daily challenge, or jump into a quick random skirmish. Climb the worldwide rankings for total stages cleared and daily high scores.

No fluff, no waiting — just clean, snappy strategy you can play in a few minutes or sink hours into.
