CubeQuiz is a relaxing 3D spatial-reasoning puzzle. Two sandy voxel blocks fit together when one slides down and the other slides up to form a solid box. Rotate the pieces, read their shapes, and decide which ones lock perfectly together. Progress through four puzzle packs of increasing difficulty, then test yourself in endless Challenge mode. No timers, no pressure — just you and your mental rotation skills.
