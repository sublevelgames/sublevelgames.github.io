A minimalist puzzle game where you connect colored dots to fill the entire grid. Draw lines between matching colors, navigate through color zones and directional arrows, and solve increasingly challenging puzzles!
Perfect for quick brain training sessions or relaxing puzzle solving. With 30 handcrafted levels plus endless procedurally generated challenges, Color Zip offers hours of engaging gameplay.

Features:
- 30 carefully designed tutorial levels
- Endless mode with infinite puzzles
- Color constraint zones and directional arrows
- Hint system for when you're stuck
- Daily rewards
- Clean, modern interface
- Relaxing gameplay with satisfying mechanics

How to Play
Basic Rules:
- Connect dots of the same color by drawing lines
- Lines cannot cross or overlap
- Fill every cell on the grid to complete the level
- Numbers on dots show remaining moves for that color

Special Elements:
- Color Zones (🎨): Your line must pass through cells marked with specific colors
- Direction Arrows (➡️): Lines must exit these cells in the indicated direction

Tips:
- Plan your path carefully - dead ends will block your progress
- Use the hint button (💡) when you need help
- Tap "Reset" to restart the current level
- Watch for numbers on dots to plan longer paths first
