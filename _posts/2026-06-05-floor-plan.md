Floor Plan is a minimalist logic puzzle inspired by architectural blueprints.

Each cell on the grid shows a number — that number tells you exactly how many walls must touch it from the four sides. Your job is to fill in the missing walls so every number in the layout is satisfied.

Simple rules. Surprisingly deep puzzles.
