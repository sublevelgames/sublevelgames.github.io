Arrow Escape 3D is a clean, satisfying puzzle game played on a rotating 3D cube. Colorful arrows are scattered across every face — tap one and it slides forward and escapes off the edge. But only if the path ahead is clear!
Look at the whole cube, plan your order, and clear every arrow off the board. Some arrows even travel across the cube's edges onto neighboring faces, so the angle you're looking from is part of the puzzle. Tap a blocked arrow and you'll lose a heart — run out and the level resets.

Simple to learn, tricky to master. One-finger controls, no timers, no pressure — just you, the cube, and that perfect "everything clicks into place" feeling.
