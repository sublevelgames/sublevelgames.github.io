Neural Break is an isometric puzzle game where you toggle interconnected nodes to turn them all OFF — or power up the ones that need to stay ON.

Each puzzle features a grid of nodes linked by edges. Tap a node to flip it and its neighbors. Master four node types with unique behaviors to crack every circuit.
