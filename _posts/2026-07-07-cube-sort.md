CubeSort is a colorful 3D tap-and-sort puzzle. Each level is a floating sculpture built from patterned tiles — cubes, spheres, stairs, arches, and hundreds of surprising shapes. Rotate it freely, find the tiles that match the open buckets, and tap them away before your tray overflows.
